FEATURES

StepGuide was designed to be simple. Many systems today are complex and try to do too much. To appreciate the simplicity, create a free account and build your own StepGuide in minutes. No credit card needed, free trial for 15 days.

1 Stop

Imagine your entire process in one collaboration platform, eliminating the need for unorganized paperwork & multi-stream communications via phone, texts, emails, meetings, etc.

Step by Step

Create process templates (StepGuides) that contain clear step-by-step instructions in bite-size chunks. Then you can copy your template to create a personal StepGuide for your client to follow, and you can modify anytime along the way.

Revision Control

Standardize, manage and track your documents, forms, licenses, accredidations, etc, to ensure that your client always has the latest version.

Progress Dashboard

Start your day with a simple real-time overview of all StepGuides.  See where your clients are stuck. See the steps that are awaiting your action.

Encrypted Data

Emailing confidential documents is not safe. The StepGuide platform allows uploads via a secure HTTPS connection. Documents are stored in our encryped database, powered by Google Cloud.
